OUR SERVICES

Reference Service

Our Reference Service is designed to provide professional assistance to all library users in finding accurate and reliable information. Our specialized librarians are available to guide you through complex database searches, literature reviews, and citations. Whether you are working on a simple assignment or a high-level research project, we provide the expertise needed to navigate the vast sea of information. We offer one-on-one consultations to help you define your research questions and find the most relevant sources. This service also includes guidance on how to use various academic tools and software effectively. We are committed to fostering your independent research skills while providing a safety net of expert knowledge. Our reference desk is strategically located at the center of the library for easy access. Experience the quality of scholarly support that only FAKHLIB can provide for your academic journey.

Circulation Service

The Circulation Service manages the lifecycle of our physical collections, including borrowing, returning, and renewing materials. We utilize a state-of-the-art automated system to ensure that the process is seamless and user-friendly for all university members. Students can easily check the availability of books through our online catalog and reserve items that are currently in high demand. Our circulation team is dedicated to maintaining the organization of our shelves so that you can find what you need quickly. We also manage the university's library membership, ensuring that every student has access to our resources from day one. Flexible loan periods and an automated notification system help you stay on top of your borrowed items. We strive to make the physical handling of books as efficient as possible through our modern self-return kiosks. The heart of our library's accessibility lies in our efficient circulation management. Let us help you keep your academic resources flowing smoothly.

Information Retrieval

Our Information Retrieval service empowers users to access a wide array of digital resources from anywhere in the world. We provide a unified search portal that connects you to millions of articles, journals, and e-books from global publishers. Our technical team works tirelessly to ensure that our retrieval systems are fast, accurate, and secure. We offer advanced search features that allow you to filter results by date, subject, author, and peer-review status. This service is essential for modern academic work, where digital resources are just as important as physical books. We provide continuous training on how to optimize your search queries to find hidden gems in our electronic databases. Our goal is to ensure that no relevant piece of information is ever out of your reach. We also provide access to specialized databases that are unique to our institution's research focus. Master the art of information gathering with our powerful retrieval tools.

Inter-Library Loan (Silang Koleksi)

Inter-Library Loan (ILL) is a collaborative service that expands our collection far beyond our physical walls. If we do not own a specific material you need, we will work with our partner institutions to borrow it for you. This service demonstrates our commitment to the global sharing of knowledge and academic cooperation. We handle all the logistics and costs associated with transporting the materials to our campus for your convenience. This is particularly useful for specialized research fields where local resources might be limited or rare. Our network includes some of the most prestigious university libraries in the region, ensuring a high success rate for your requests. Most requests for digital copies are fulfilled within 48 hours, keeping your research momentum going without interruption. We believe that access to information should never be limited by geography or institutional boundaries. Use our ILL service to tap into a wider world of academic resources today.

Similarity Check (Turnitin)

To uphold the highest levels of academic integrity, FAKHLIB provides a professional Similarity Check service using Turnitin software. This tool helps students and faculty ensure that their written work is original and properly cited before final submission. By comparing your documents against a massive global database of academic papers and web content, we help you identify potential issues. Our librarians provide detailed reports and guidance on how to interpret similarity percentages effectively. This service is not just about catching errors; it is about teaching the importance of original thought and proper attribution. We offer regular workshops on academic writing and the ethical use of information to supplement this service. Using Turnitin is a standard practice at our university to protect the reputation of our scholars and the institution. Ensure your academic work meets the highest standards by utilizing our similarity check service. Your commitment to original scholarship starts with a simple check here at FAKHLIB.
